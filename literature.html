Wrote on governance, ethics and philosophy

Budhbhushanam (बुधभूषणम्)
Language: Sanskrit Type: Treatise (Granth) on Politics & Statecraft Description: A detailed text on governance, administration, and military strategies. Contains verses on morality, dharma, and justice. Showcases Sambhaji Maharaj’s deep knowledge of Hindu scriptures.

Nayikabhed (नायिकाभेद)
Language: Sanskrit Type: Poetic Work on Different Types of Women in Literature Description: Describes different types of heroines (Nayikas) in classical literature. Inspired by ancient Sanskrit aesthetics & poetry. Showcases his literary prowess and deep understanding of emotions & art.

Saatsatak (सातसतक) (Disputed Work)
Language: Marathi (Some parts) Type: Poetic Collection Description: A collection of 700 verses, some of which are attributed to Sambhaji Maharaj. Written in Marathi & Sanskrit, reflecting his multilingual expertise. Some historians debate his authorship, but it showcases his poetic influence.
